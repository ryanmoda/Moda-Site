Mini Cove is an award winning, line voltage, cove lighting system which is now available in DMX-512 controllable RGB. With the same miniature 1 5/16” (33mm) fixture size, Mini Cove RGB delivers beautiful, saturated colors perfect for cove or accent applications. Accepting standard 8-bit DMX, our on-board firmware and microprocessor uses a non-linear dimming algorithm to produce industry leading dimming curves that are amazingly smooth.

Our fixtures utilize the most advanced Tricolor LEDs in the world to produce vibrant, uniform colors. Simplicity is key to an overall frustration-free installation process. No additional control boxes, bridges, adapters or converter are required making specification and installation fast and trouble free... The fixture incorporates an integral, patented, tool-less self-locking bracket which slides into any position and rotates 170 degrees. Built-in male & female connectors with thumb latch connections make for fast installation time. With Quality American Made electronic components and Lightweight aluminum construction, durability and excellent thermal characteristics are surely provided. Integral end to end thumb latch connectors with simple leader and jumper cables complete a rapid installation. A specialized curved polymer optical lens combined with a cleverly designed Light Chamber delivers a light bending, smooth 200 degree beam pattern perfect for cove installations with no color over angle, no striations, inter locking fields of light and no dark spots.

Choose from RGB, RGBA or RGBW fixtures. RGBW fixtures are available in High CRI 2700K, 3000K, 3500K or 4000K. These fixtures benefit with high R1-R15 values and excellent TM30 results for excellent white light cove illumination. Combine white light and RGB dies to create soft pastel colors. 2700K is an excellent example resembling incandescent illumination producing colors that are crisp, sharp and ideal for all applications which require a high quality incandescent atmosphere. 3000K has often been described as one of the best looking in the industry. 3500K has become our standard go to temperature, not being too neutral with a hint of warmth. 4000K is perfectly suiting for an alert dynamic in office and retail settings.

Accessories include a full series of Controllers, Moda dual power and data cables, DMX cables, Moda DMX splitters, Moda DMX wiring Hubs and an easy to install mounting track. Choose from 1ft or 4ft sections.
