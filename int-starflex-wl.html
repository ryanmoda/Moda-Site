Our first and original flexible linear lighting product. Designed in 2005, STARFLEX was originally called MRT01 (Moda Ribbon Tape 01) and has become one of our flagship products in our flexible linear series. STARFLEX is only available as a single bin. Which means every reel looks and performs the same. Single 2 Step MacAdam Ellipse centered on the Planckian Locus. Rolled-Annealed Copper PCB for even heat dissipation and long life. Tested to UL & CSA Standards by ETL. Tested to LM-79-08 & LM80-08. IES file available. See XTRUSION® for a multitude of options.
