Ecoflex is one of our original series of flexible solid-state lighting solutions. This high efficacy (99 lm/w) cove/cabinet illuminator is made from pure rolled copper giving it excellent thermal management properties and perfect cooling for the on board LEDs. Single lifetime bin ensures that every reel is the same today, light color and performance will never change and will be the same tomorrow. Easy scissor cutting every 2” (50mm), 3M self adhesive backing, leader cables and jumper cables and easy push to fit connectors makes Ecoflex a simple and fast installation.
