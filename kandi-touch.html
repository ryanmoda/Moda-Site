Kandi Touch

Kandi Touch TPC VLC LPC LPC X
Product Guide
Installs

Kandi Touch is a wall mounted DMX512 controller. Its touch sensitive color display allows for easy selection of up to 500 scenes across 10 zones. It has multi universe control and can easily manage color dimming or speed of a scene through the color wheel with a simple touch.

Kandi Touch can be hooked up to an Ethernet connection for easy control from a computer, smart phone or other device with WiFi access.
