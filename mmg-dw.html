Mini Graze Interior is an award-winning, line voltage, graze lighting system. This patented series is now available in DMX controllable Dynamic White. Ideal for graze applications where high-quality, tunable white is a requirement. Advanced Moda microprocessors allow the fixtures to be controlled via standard DMX512 signals. This, together with a non-linear dimming algorithm results in ultra-smooth transitions and dimming across the white light spectrum.

Moda Light utilizes the industries most advanced LEDs that are strictly single bin, (2-step MacAdam Ellipse), ensuring uniform and consistent white light. Moda Light’s intelligent and simplified systems do not require any additional power/data enablers, and with 100-277V line voltage as a standard, Moda Mini Graze Interior is a fast and simple product to install. ..

The fixture incorporates an integral, patented, toolless self-locking bracket which can slide along the body of the fixture and be rotated 170 degrees. The built-in male and female connectors have thumb latch connectors resulting in a fast, simple installation. Constructed from lightweight, durable aluminum with excellent thermal characteristics, our fixtures are also integrated with quality American made electronic components to be some of the highest quality fixtures available. The specialized polymer optical lens combined with a cleverly designed Light Chamber delivers a light bending 200-degree beam pattern, perfect for graze installations with no color over angle, no striations, or dark spots.

With the ability to mix our own phosphors, Moda Mini Graze Interior maintains a High Color Rendering Index of 96-98 and is R1-R15 tested to TM30-15 with values in excess of 90 thus creating beautiful illumination.

Mini Graze Interior Dynamic White allows the ability to control color temperature within the same small fixture. Research has shown that adjusting color temperature will affect mood. The sun naturally cycles through various color temperatures every day. Cooler color temperatures stimulate a focused, alert state and increase productivity. Warmer color temperatures contribute to a calmer, relaxing environment and data shows it enhances melatonin production at night. Dynamic White is able to mimic daylighting and the natural circadian rhythms that accompany it. Specifiers are encouraging the use of these platforms to enhance education, healthcare, retail, hospitality, and even correctional facilities. Yellows and gold finishes are enhanced with warmer color temperatures. Silvers appear brighter with cooler color temperatures. While enhancing the ambiance of space using Dynamic White is an important aspect of the technology, the ability to address and control each 1ft individually also allows the user to create various scenes within the white light installation–for example, color temperature crossfades, chasing scenes, and sparkles.

Choose from Dynamic White (2200K-6500K), Dynamic Warm (1800K-3000K) or Dynamic Cool (3500K-6500K). 1800K is candlelight–perfect for hospitality. 2700K is an excellent example of resembling incandescent illumination. Colors are crisp and sharp, ideal for all applications which require a high-quality incandescent look. The Moda Light 3000K is often described as one of the best looking 3000K in the industry. 3500K has now become our standard temperature. 4000K is perfectly suited for offices and retail. Moda has been using a single binning process since 2006, meaning every fixture will have the same CCT. Choose from 1ft or 4ft sections. Accessories include a DMX wiring hub for fast hassle-free wiring and an easy to install mounting track.
