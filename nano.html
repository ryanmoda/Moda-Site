MODA NANO COVE does not have a driver and dims from 100-0% with electronic low voltage (ELV) controls. Dimming is synchronized with all connected fixtures our award winning MODA SYNERGY technology. Use the MODA BRIDGE to control this series with 0-10V controls.

Our patent pending new dimming technology enables our fixtures to dim so precise and to be flawless at the low end trim of a dimming system. Combining our advanced firmware with intelligent micro processors and dimming chips, this technology creates a 100-0% dimming curve, smooth, no noise and flicker free and allows our lighting systems to be completely synchronized.

This proprietary ASIC – Application-Specific Integrated Circuit – technology eliminates the need for a separate driver. It incorporates a forward-thinking power-on-board system, which replicates the same functionality of an LED board and driver and enables the fixture size to decrease.

MODA NANO COVE ensures quality and convenience with its easy, integral thumb-latch connectors that allows for rapid, toolless, plug and play installation and continuous runs up to 120ft. Plugs only fit one way, ensuring a secure electrical connection every time. Conventional wiring with an input of 120V direct from the dimming system creates a powerful lighting system, saving time and money.

Easy Installation featuring

Integral male and female thumb latch connectors provide a simple tool-less rapid fit connection between fixtures, saving time and installation costs. Leader Cables and Jumper Cables are also connected to fixtures using this tool-less technology.
