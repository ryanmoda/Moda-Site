Project Manager

September 26 , 2021 Las Vegas, NV

MODA Light is an innovative architectural lighting company and is currently seeking a Operations Manager to join its team. As part of the MODA Light team, the Operations Manager’s primary responsibility is to be the primary liaison between the VP of Operations and the direct-reporting team and be accountable for overseeing the day-to-day affairs including warehouse, fulfillment, receiving, maintenance/ facilities, and production/manufacturing with additional support to sales, technical services, and marketing.

 

Primary Functions: 

 

 • Under the direction of leadership, oversee daily operations of main facility.

 • Ensure that the company delivers excellent customer services at all times.

 • Support aspects of receiving and order fulfillment process and make every effort for efficient ‘right the first time’ processes.

 • Facilitate effective problem solving and proactive thinking while demonstrating initiative and providing suggestions for improving processes.

 • Facilitate intercompany team communication, pro-actively troubleshoot potential pitfalls, and develop new and existing business channels.

 • Make recommendations on staffing levels based on organizational needs.

 • Reinforce company policies and procedures, while maintaining a healthy working relationship across the organization.

 • Coordinate workloads, train and motivate employees, monitor associated job performance, and conduct formal reviews and corrective action if necessary.

 

Education and Experience Requirements: 

 

 • BA/BS degree required in business, science, or engineering discipline

 • 5+ years of management experience in warehouse/manufacturing/operations

 • 2-3 years’ progressive experience in lighting or manufacturing operations management

 • 2+ years of warehouse/pick/pack/ship/fulfillment experience

 • IT experience and comprehensive working knowledge of NetSuite

 • Current Forklift Certification required

 

Benefits: 

 • Salary commensurate with experience.

 • Medical, Dental, Vision and Life Insurance.

 • Paid Time Off in addition to Paid Holidays, following introductory period.

 • 401k with matching.

 

 Must be available to work from 7:30am - 4:00pm, Monday thru Friday.
