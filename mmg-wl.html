Mini Graze is an exciting brand new wall grazing lighting system built upon our award-winning, patented, Mini Cove series. It is powerful and efficient with a choice of standard output at 502 lumens per foot or high output delivering 1004 lumens per foot, all packaged in a small size of just 1 5/16in (33mm) tall fixture. Unlike other fixtures which are much larger in physical size, Mini Graze technology ensures fixtures are cool to the touch even at 1004 lumens per foot. This is achieved by designing a forward-thinking fixture with intelligent driver technology and by underdriving the most advanced LEDs in the World. Thus creating a fixture which is so cool it can be held after days of illumination. The L70 (longevity) of the fixture is extended and phosphor degradation kept to a minimum...

The onboard American dimming chipset, programmed by our Moda Software Division ensures Mini Graze delivers smooth, no noise, flicker-free and synchronized dimming down to 0%. Choose from either ELV or 0-10V direct dimming fixtures. Unlike other fixtures in the marketplace, there is no need to purchase additional adapters, converters, dimming boxes or control modules. Just use conventional wiring direct from the dimmer to create a powerful lighting system, saving time and money. Link any combination of 6in, 1ft, 4ft, and 8ft fixtures and every fixture will dim together smoothly, synchronized from 100% to 0%. With no minimum run lengths, just one 6in fixture will dim seamlessly to 0%.

Wall wash any indoor application with its integral linear optic of 30° x 100° for a perfect asymmetric beam pattern with no color over angle, no striations and no dark spots up to 20ft (6m). With Mini Graze the installer is able to achieve long single runs of up to 240 feet, decreasing the number of wiring circuits required in an installation. Another step forward in this category of small-sized fixtures is the Universal power input. We designed a small Moda Micro Driver specific for Mini Graze enabling it to accept 100-277V AC 50/60Hz, thus decreasing the number of SKUs needed to be held by any of our Worldwide distributors and giving the installer a choice of power inputs from one fixture.

With the ability to mix our own phosphors we are able to make a revolutionary new standard for Moda offering a High Color Rendering Index of 96-98 with LEDs centered on The Planckian Locus for superior illumination. Our fixtures are R1-R15 tested to TM30-15 with values in excess of 90, creating beautiful graze illumination. Moda has been using a single binning process since 2006, meaning every fixture will have the same CCT. A specialized curved polymer optical lens combined with a cleverly designed Light Chamber delivers a light bending, smooth 200 degree beam pattern perfect for graze installations with no color over angle, no striations, interlocking fields of light and no dark spots. Choose from our standard white light temperatures of 1800K, 2200K, 2700K, 3000K, 3500K, 4000K, and 6500K. 2200K is ideal for hospitality applications with its rich warm tones. Reds, Blues, and Greens look clean and sharp thanks to our own Phosphor mix and High CRI. 2200K no longer looks dull, dirty or “muddy”. 2700K is an excellent example of incandescent looking illumination. Colors are crisp and sharp. It is ideal for all applications which require a high-quality incandescent look. Our 3000K fixture has often been described as one of the best looking 3000K in the industry. 3500K is the color that has become our standard temperature. 4000K is perfect for offices and retail. Our High CRI models create great graze illumination no matter what color temperature is chosen.

The fixture incorporates an integral, patented, toolless self-locking bracket which slides into any position and turns 170 degrees. It has built-in male and female connectors with thumb latch connections for fast installation time, lightweight aluminum construction for durability and excellent thermal characteristics, as well as quality American made electronic components. Mini Graze Interior is available in a tiny 6-inch size (15cm) for tight turning coves, or to finish a small space on the end of a run. 1ft, 4ft and 8ft lengths are for fast installation saving expensive labor installation costs and achieving long runs of quality illumination in rapid time.

Tested by Intertek to UL1598 for use in USA and Canada. CE Approved. WEE. RoHS Compliant. TM-30-15 Tested. US Patent No. D787,112 and D783,890.
